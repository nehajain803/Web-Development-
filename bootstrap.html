Designers Luisa and Lillian Parrado integrated their interest in good design and the desire for growing plants into awesome garden modules. The twin sisters created this modular element with geometric shape that serves as a stand for potted plants.

The element is made of white iron tubes connected together in a geometric shape and the shelves are made of concrete. The entire unit has industrial note, but also a fresh look because of the white color and shape that makes it attractive for modern interiors.

The shelves can fit in the living room with potted flowers and a few books or on the terrace wall. Their modern form and application allows part of the interior seem like a small urban garden, bringing nature and freshness in the home.
